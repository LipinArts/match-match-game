Match-Match Game

Match-Match Game is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Rules

  • All pictures on a cards are hidden
  • When you select the card she are turn face up
  • Select two cards to try to match the pictures
  • If the pictures match they will remain upside down face up
  • If dont match they are turned face down again
  • The game continues until all the pictures match up
